GSAP Sandbox

Ten exercises, one per card. The elements and buttons are ready — the animations are not. Open gsap-animations/scripts/app.js and write them yourself, exercise by exercise.

01 · Your first tween — gsap.to()

The atom of GSAP: animate any property of any element to a value.

02 · from() & fromTo() — entrances

Animate from a starting state, perfect for elements appearing on the page.

03 · Eases — the personality of motion

Same tween, different ease. Pick one and replay to feel the difference.

04 · Staggers — one tween, many targets

Animate a whole grid with a single tween and a stagger object.

05 · Timelines — choreographing sequences

Chain tweens with precise overlaps instead of juggling delay values.

06 · quickTo() — buttery mouse following

Goal: the ball smoothly chases your cursor around the arena.

07 · TextPlugin — typewriter effects

Animate the text content of an element, character by character.

Solve exercise 07 and this sentence gets typed over...

08 · ScrollTrigger — scroll-linked animation

Goal: scrolling this card up and down through the viewport drags the box back and forth — the scrollbar becomes the playhead.

09 · Draggable — grab the box

Goal: the box can be dragged around, can't leave the stage, and snaps to a grid on release.

10 · Flip — animating layout changes

Flip records where elements are, lets you rearrange the DOM however you want, then animates from the old layout to the new one.
